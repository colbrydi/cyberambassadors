Practice Examples for Complex Communication

 Mon 06 August 2018 by Dirk Colbry

We are excited to present some of our newest CyberAmbassador Curriculum developed specifically for the 2018 ACI-REF Virtual residency. We have been given a 90 minute slot to introduce the topic of communicating complex topics. Below is a link to our slides and links to the practice examples:

  • Link to the presentation slides
  • Link to the Practice Examples

One exciting thing about trying out this activity at ACI-REF VR is that a large population of the participants are going to log in remotely. We are going to use this opportunity to try out groups of three using Zoom breakout rooms. We are really curious to see how well this type of practice exercise works remotely. This could potentially be a way to save costs on future CyberAmbassador trainings.
